About PastelBeans®

Founded in 1997 by a group of artist friends who share the love and passion for coffee, is a coffee house chain in 7 locations around the world: England, France, Japan, Germany, K.S.A, U.A.E and the U.S and it is ambicious to expand and reach out with its handcrafted, high quality coffee to more countries by the end of this decade.

With our artistic touches comes a delicate dedication to bring you the most satisfying experience. Our pastel-y colorful pallets of fine beverages will ensure you an unforgetable journey to tastefulness and a new meaning for joy.

We've been around the world collecting the most excellent of beans, shared stories and learned experiences, to live up to our name to a level of perfection. We believe that not all choices in life can be made, but we can always choose our coffee, and our ultimate goal is that you choose ours.

Our Beans

Just like how artists carefully choose the colors they want to paint their pictures with, we as well carefully pick our beans and make sure that every step of the process of bringing them to you is carried out with utmost care and professionality, since we believe that it all affects the final taste.

Fresh, hand brewed beans from around the world are the soul of our coffee, hand picked with delicacy, tasted and approved by our experts, all up to your satisfaction.
